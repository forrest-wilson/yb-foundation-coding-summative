How do I work?

Hi there! I see you want to know how this application works. The TL;DR is enter some info, click some buttons and *poof*, we'll recommend the perfect options for your travels!

A more 'technical' version of the above is:

1. Enter the route you would like to take.

2. Enter how many people are coming along on this journey.

3. Enter the number of days you'd like this vehicle for.

4. Select your preferred vehicle from the provided options.
